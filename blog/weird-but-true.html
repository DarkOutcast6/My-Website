Bigger Than a Ton!?

The three most common measurements greater than a ton are megatons, gigatons and kilotons, gigaton being the largest and kilotons the smallest. All three of which are mainly used for measuring solar masses and/or large masses of TNT. A kiloton is equal to a unit of explosive power equal to 1,000 tons of TNT.
