Jerboas

Jerboas are small, nocturnal rodents, often known for their long legs. You can find them in parts of Northern Africa, Arabia, and Asia. They tend to live for three to six years and have a whopping top speed of fifteen miles per hour! Jerboas have exceptional hearing, thanks to their enormous ears and surprisingly, they do not drink water! All sources of liquid and hydration come directly from their food, not unlike other desert animals. They can also leap an amazing six feet high in the air.
